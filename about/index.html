About Iranga Basnayake

Senior System Analyst & AI Engineer with 20+ years of enterprise impact.

Iranga Basnayake is a senior systems and AI engineer based in Kandy, Sri Lanka, with two decades of experience building production software for global apparel manufacturing — one of the world’s largest manufacturers depends on systems Iranga has architected and shipped.

What I do

I lead automation, mobile, and AI initiatives at MAS Holdings — designing and shipping enterprise systems that move products, people, and decisions faster. My software runs on factory floors, in warehouses, and on the phones of thousands of operators across multiple countries. I work end-to-end: backend architecture, mobile applications, AI integration, and production rollout.

Areas of expertise

  • On-premises Large Language Models — Ollama, Open WebUI, RAG pipelines, secure enterprise AI assistants that never send a token to the cloud.
  • Enterprise process automation — Django and Python systems that replace paper, Excel, and email workflows with auditable software.
  • React Native mobile applications — production apps for warehouse operations, attendance tracking, audits, and field workforce management.
  • System architecture & integration — connecting ERP, IoT devices, mobile apps, and AI services into one coherent platform.
  • Privacy-first product design — DRM, watermarking, on-device AI, hardened mobile shells (see VaultCast).

Education

MSc in Computer Science — Cardiff Metropolitan University (UK), First Class Honours.
BSc in Software Engineering — London Metropolitan University (UK), Second Upper Class.

Career trajectory

Two decades, one mission: better systems. I’ve held roles from network administrator and IT assistant through to system administrator, system analyst, and now senior system analyst — always shipping software that solves real operational problems for the business.

Selected projects

  • VaultCast — privacy-first video conferencing with DRM, on-device recording detection, and dynamic watermarking.
  • MWash — chemical inventory and recipe management for textile manufacturing.
  • ILAB (Matrix Digital Lab) — digital lab for textile quality control.

Where I’m based

Kandy, Sri Lanka — working globally. I respond fastest via hello@iranga.dev and work comfortably across time zones with European, North American, and Asia-Pacific clients.

Open to projects and the right full-time role. I work on enterprise software, on-premises AI, and privacy-first product engineering.
